🎮 How to Play Paper.io

Claim the map by filling spaces as your block! Avoid enemies and block other players to win.

  • Move your block around the map to claim territory
  • Cover more of the map than your opponents to win
  • Don't touch your own trail or you get eliminated
  • If you touch an opponent's trail, they get eliminated
  • Avoid being touched by other players
  • The block follows your mouse or touch input
