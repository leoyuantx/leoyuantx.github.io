how to play: paper.io



Paper io is a game where you are a block of any skin
and you run around the map filling more spaces as you go.
don't get killed! you get killed by someone touching your line
if you touch your own line you get killed too
you can also touch other people's lines to get them killed.
to win you have to cover the whole map.
