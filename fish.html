Lava Island Arena

Battle on a small island in the middle of a lava river. Defeat all AI fighters to win.

If you win, you earn 100-500 tokens. Use tokens in the shop to buy characters and abilities.

AI factions also fight each other, and the stronger your loadout is, the stronger the AI becomes.

Controls: WASD/Arrows move, Space attacks/jumps, Shift dashes.
