🎮 How to Play Tic Tac 4

Get 4 in a row to win! It's like Tic Tac Toe, but bigger and more challenging.

  • Play on a 4x4 grid (16 squares instead of 9)
  • Get 4 of your marks in a row to win (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)
  • Take turns with your opponent placing X's and O's
  • Block your opponent from getting 4 in a row
  • More squares = more strategy and fun!
