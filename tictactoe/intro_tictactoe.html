🎮 How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Get 3 in a row to win! It's simple, strategic, and fun.

  • Player 1 plays as X, Player 2 plays as O
  • Take turns placing your marks on a 3x3 grid
  • First to get 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • Block your opponent from getting 3 in a row
  • If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw
